The Wrap Shack is a kitchen and bar located in the heart of Philadelphia. It is owned by Scott Hockfield a prowd father and owner of Bar 18 and Morea which are also located in center city. The wrap shack has been open for 10 years and has been in 3 different locations accross center city PA. Wrap Shack is an American-style kitchen and bar with locations in Rittenhouse and Midtown Village, Philadelphia. With signature wraps and sandwiches, snacks and apps, and a rotating selection of bottles, drafts, and cocktails, Wrap Shack is your local spot to catch the game or simply have a good time with friends.
